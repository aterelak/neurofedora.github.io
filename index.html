Welcome to the NeuroFedora blog. NeuroFedora is an initiative to provide a ready to use Fedora based Free/Open Source Software platform for neuroscience. We believe that similar to Free Software, science should be free for all to use, share, modify, and study. The use of Free Software also aids reproducibility, data sharing, and collaboration in the research community. By making the tools used in the scientific process easier to use, NeuroFedora aims to take a step to enable this ideal.

This blog is used to provide updates and share information related to NeuroFedora. Documentation on using and contributing to NeuroFedora is hosted at https://neuro.fedoraproject.org.
